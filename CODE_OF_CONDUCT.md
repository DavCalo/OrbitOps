# Code of conduct

OrbitOps is committed to a respectful, professional, and harassment-free collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, precise, and considerate.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome questions and different experience levels.
- Respect privacy, attribution, and project security boundaries.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about scope and safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and publication of private or security-sensitive information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `calo.davide02@gmail.com`. Reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.
